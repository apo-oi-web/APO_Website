Leadership


    Leadership is one of the three cardinal principals of Alpha Phi Omega. As a national organization Alpha Phi Omega strives to develop our members as leaders through service to others. This approach builds the leadership skills of our members while fulfilling a need on campuses and in communities, therefore leaving a lasting impact on our nation.

    Alpha Phi Omega develops the leadership skills of its members through shared leadership. Our members have the opportunity to serve as a chapter officer or to plan and execute any number of volunteer projects and social programs, as well as have the chance to participate in a variety of leadership development programs sponsored by Alpha Phi Omega.

LEADS

     Alpha Phi Omega develops the leadership skills of its members through shared leadership. Our members have the opportunity to serve as a chapter officer or to plan and execute any number of volunteer projects and social programs, as well as have the chance to participate in a variety of leadership development programs sponsored by Alpha Phi Omega.

     We have many elected and appointed officer and chair positions within the chapter. Members in their first semester may hold chair positions such as intramural chair, weekly service chair, and web chair. Members in their second semester may hold chair or officer positions. We also host committees that any members and pledges may join. Nationally, there are leadership development classes offered to members and pledges of the fraternity either online or in person. These include the 5-part APO LEADS program and APO IMPACT webinars.

Committees

     At the WPI Chapter of Alpha Phi Omega, almost all aspects are organized by students directly. Our chapter utilizes committees to avoid overwhelming a single student with planning any given event, or completing a specific task. As part of our active requirements, members are asked to join a committee and participate in it by attending meetings and working on assignments. Committees are headed by executive board members and elected chairs. Current committees include Merit Badge University Planning (MBU), Web, Chapter Image (PR), Formal/Semiformal, Fundraising, Recruitment, and Rivalry.
