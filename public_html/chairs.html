Diversity and Inclusion Chair

The Diversity and Inclusion Chair reports to the President. They are in charge of making sure our brotherhood is open to everyone. They plan events that align with our core principles while integrating and focusing on issues that minority groups face everyday.


Weekly Service Chair

The Weekly Service Chair(s) reports to the Service Vice President(s). They are in charge of planning weekly services for the chapter.


Core Service Chairs

The Core Service Chair(s) reports to the Service Vice President(s). They are in charge of planning and running the annual services; these annual services include Merit Badge University, Raffle for Relief, and Badge Blast.


Recruitment Chair

The Recruitment Chair reports to the Membership Vice President(s). They are in charge of overseeing and planning recruitment and gathering feedback following recruitment.


Retention Chair

The Retention Chair reports to the Membership Vice President(s). They are in charge of planning and hosting Brother Appreciation Week and encouraging brother engagement.


Alumni Relations Chair

The Alumni Relations Chair reports to the Membership Vice President(s). They are in charge of maintaining contact with Alumni of the chapter.


Semiformal/Formal Chair

The Semiformal/Formal Chair(s) reports to the Fellowship Vice President(s). They are in charge of planning Semiformal/Formal.


Intramurals Chair

The Intramurals Chair reports to the Fellowship Vice President(s). They are in charge of being the captain of APO-OI’s intramural teams and overseeing events.


Fundraising Chair

The Fundraising Chair reports to the Treasurer. They are in charge of coordinating fundraising for the chapter.


Web Chair

The Web Chair reports to the Public Relations Officer. They are in charge of maintaining and developing APO’s web-based assets.


Heads of Houses

The Heads of Houses report to the Sergeant at Arms. They are in charge of planning house events, such as services and fellowships.
