Directions
From the East and the North

Proceed west on I-290 into Worcester. Take Exit 18 (Lincoln Square, Rt. 9), turn right at end of ramp, then an immediate right before next traffic light. At the next light, proceed straight through, bearing to the right on Salisbury Street. At the WPI sign, turn left onto Boynton Street, then right into the WPI parking lot.

From the South and the West

Proceed east on I-290 into Worcester. Take Exit 17 (Lincoln Square, Rt. 9), turn left at end of ramp, follow Rt. 9 west through Lincoln Square, straight onto Highland Street, then right onto Boynton Street. Travel one block, and cross Institute Road. Turn left into the WPI parking lot.

Go up the stair case to the top of the hill. Take a left at the top of the stairs and enter the first building before you on the right (Salisbury Labs).
