All Weekend

  • Video, Board & Outdoor games available for use throughout the weekend. If the weather cooperates, startup a game of Frisbee or volleyball on the quad! If not, we have screens for videogames as well.
  • Kids' Room stocked with games and simple crafts (adults must accomany children)

Friday Evening

  • Check-in
  • Relax & Reconnect over Board Games: Kick back on Friday night and play a few board games with old & new friends. Games will continue to be available throught the weekend.
  • Make a letter shirt to keep or give: Bring a T-shirt to decorate with APO letters as a souvenir for yourself, or pass on to the fall pledge class, or bring two to do both!

Saturday

All Day

  • Campus Tours: Join WPI Tour guides to see how the WPI campus has grown over the years
  • Family Trees: Come together to recreate your APO family trees through big & little pairings. Trees will be displayed throughout the weekend.

Daytime Activities

  • Check-in
  • Welcome & Speed Networking: Saturday morning introduction for the day and a chance to meet fellow brothers with a quick activity
  • Service: Park Cleanup: Help beautify Worcester by cleaning up the WPI-adjacent Institute park. Please bring weather appropriate clothes you won't mind getting dirty if participating.
  • Service: Dry Soup Kits: Prepare shelf-stable "just add water" soup starters for donation to the Mustard Seed Soup Kitchen
  • Lunch on your own
  • Alumni Association & Chapter Update: Find out what the alumni association and undergraduates are doing these days, how to join the alumni association, or how you can stay involved.
  • Remember When: Reminisce with classmates, tell stories of your past, and see how the chapter has changed through the years. Bring mementos to show others or browse chapter scrapbooks & photos.
  • APO Trivia: How much do you remember about the fraternity & WPI? Form a team and enter the trivia. Winners will be announced at the banquet.

Evening Activites

  • Banquet: Saturday night's banquet will take place in the WPI Campus Center and will feature brother Wes Mott, a longtime chapter advisor, as keynote speaker. During the banquet, the anniversary weekend awards (trivia prizes, 50/50 raffle winners, etc) will be announced. We will conclude the evening with a rededication ceremony followed by music and dancing.
  • 50/50 Raffle: To support the active brothers attending this year's National Convention in Chicago, IL
  • Rededication

Sunday

  • Explore Worcester solor or with a group: Carpool or meet up with brothers at Worcester Art Museum, EcoTarium, or Purgatory Chasm. Trip boards posted during weekend to facilitate groups and destinations
