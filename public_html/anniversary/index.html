Celebrating 50 years of Alpha Phi Omega at WPI!

APO-OI's 50th anniversary was celebrated during the weekend of October 24-26, 2014, with a variety of service and fellowship activities for active brothers, alumni, and their families.

Saturday evening featured a banquet and a rededication ceremony. Through the night, we heard from brothers through the years as well as brother Wes Mott, an advisor who has been serving our chapter for two decades.
